    "# Grüneisen Parameters\n",
    "\n",
    "\n",
    "Gruneisen parameters, the strain derivatives of phonon frequencies can be rewritten in terms of the strain derivatives of dynamic matrices.\n",
    "\n",
    "$$\n",
    "\\gamma_{i}(\\textbf{q}) = - \\frac{V}{\\omega_{i}(\\textbf{q})} \\frac{\\partial \\omega_{i}(\\textbf{q})}{\\partial V}\n",
    "% = - \\frac{1}{\\omega_{i}(\\textbf{q})} \\frac{\\partial \\omega_{i}(\\textbf{q})}{\\partial \\epsilon}\n",
    "= - \\frac{\\partial \\ln \\omega_{i}(\\textbf{q})}{\\partial \\epsilon}\n",
    "= - \\frac{1}{2} \\frac{\\partial \\ln (\\omega_{i}(\\textbf{q}))^2}{\\partial \\epsilon}\n",
    "= - \\frac{1}{2(\\omega_{i}(\\textbf{q}))^2} \\frac{\\partial (\\omega_{i}(\\textbf{q}))^2}{\\partial \\epsilon}\n",
    "\\\\\n",
    "= - \\frac{1}{2(\\omega_{i}(\\textbf{q}))^2} \n",
    "    \\frac{\\partial \\left<W_i(\\textbf{q})\\right|\\hat{D}(\\textbf{q})\\left|W_i(\\textbf{q})\\right>}{\\partial \\epsilon}\n",
    "= - \\frac{1}{2(\\omega_{i}(\\textbf{q}))^2} \n",
    "    \\left\\langle W_i(\\textbf{q})\\middle\\vert\n",
    "    \\frac{\\partial \\hat{D}(\\textbf{q})}{\\partial \\epsilon}\n",
    "    \\middle\\vert W_i(\\textbf{q}) \\right\\rangle\n",
    "$$\n",
    "\n",
    "This can be directly measured by finite difference by straining the cell and compute the resulting dynamic matrix changes."

    "## Compute grüneisen parameters from third order force constants\n",
    "\n",
    "The strain derivative of dynamic matrices can be computed purely from 3rd order force constants tensors, \n",
    "computed from Fourier interpolation of the dynamic tensors.\n",
    "The forumla can be derived using pertubation theory:\n",
    "\n",
    "$$\n",
    "\\frac{ \\partial D{{}_{\\bar{\\textbf{q}}\\textbf{q}}^{mn} }   }{\\partial \\epsilon_{A}^N} =\n",
    "\\frac{\\sqrt{n_{a}}}{d} \n",
    "\\sum_{\\textbf{t}} \n",
    "e^{-i 2\\pi \\textbf{q}\\cdot \\textbf{t}  }\n",
    "\\sum_{\\textbf{t}_1 b_1 \\beta_1} \n",
    "\\Phi_{\\textbf{0},\\textbf{t},\\textbf{t}_1}^{m,n,(b_1,\\beta_1)}\n",
    "\\sum_{\\alpha_1} \n",
    "\\left(\\textbf{t}_{k}\\hat{\\textbf{a}} + \\mathcal{A}_{b_1} \\right)\\cdot\\textbf{e}_{\\alpha_1}\n",
    "\\langle u_{\\Gamma}^{(b_k,\\beta_k)}  | \\psi_\\Gamma^{\\alpha_k} \\rangle\n",
    "$$\n",
    "\n",
    "The rest is the same as direct measurement.\n",
    "\n",
    "Ref:\n",
    "D. C. Wallace, Thermodynamics of Crystals (Courier Corporation, New York, 1998),\n",
    "[J. Fabian and P. B. Allen, Phys. Rev. Lett. 79, 1885 (1997)](https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevLett.79.1885).\n",
    "\n",
    "The equation is also presented in our paper:\n",
    "[L. Fu, M. Kornbluth, Z. Cheng, and C. A. Marianetti, Phys. Rev. B 100, 014303 (2019)](https://doi.org/10.1103/PhysRevB.102.245104)"
   ]

Translation group

Translation group the group that preserves the translation invariance of crystal. Here we focus on finite translation group (FTG), a homomorphic mapping with the infinite translation group via periodic boundary conditions. It’s often referred to as a k-point grid or a super cell. We will refer to it as \(\hat{\mathbf{S}}_{BZ}\).
